Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What if I can't log in to my account? Ensure you're using the correct email and password. If you're still unable to log in, contact our support team through the form above.

How do I make a reservation as a customer? After logging in as a customer, you can make a reservation by navigating to the "Make a Reservation" section in your dashboard.

How do I update the inventory? Merchants can update the inventory by accessing the "Inventory" section from their dashboard and submitting updated stock levels.
